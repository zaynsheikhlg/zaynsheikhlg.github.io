The Hashemite Sayyed Family of Hejaz is a noble lineage rooted in the sacred cities of Makkah and Madinah. Descended from the family of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), the family has carried centuries of religious leadership, cultural preservation, and noble service to the holy lands.

Our great-grandfather, Shareef e Hejaz Sayyed Mohammed Kalimullah Hashmi, was a cousin of Sharif Hussein of Makkah, the leader of the Arab Revolt. His son, Sayyed Abdullah Jumman, served as an Imam of Masjid al-Nabawi for 27 years and was known for his Sufi poetry and wisdom. His grandson, Sayyed Abdul Gafoor, stood as a prince of the Hejaz and played a spiritual and political role alongside King Faisal during the crucial moments of Saudi Arabia's Islamic history.

Today, the legacy continues through Her Highness Sayyedah Ameerah Shakila and her son, His Highness Sayyed Mohammed Shamsher, who continue to uphold the values, dignity, and leadership of the Sayyed family in the modern age.

This site stands as a digital monument to their honor, history, and continuing legacy.
